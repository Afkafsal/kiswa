Mission

To be active and enthusiastic feet in upbringing of the well packed scholars by feeding them Islamic knowledge, without neglecting the importance of modernity by conducting various discourses pertaining to contemporary social scenario. KISWA helps students be updated and highly attached with society in social interference and outreach. Without overlooking the traditional ones, KISWA always stands for innovative upliftment of students that copes up with the advancements of growing developments for propagation of Islam.

Vision

We envision a united student body that thrives through collaboration, innovation, and inclusivity.
